Author Guidelines

1. Manuscript Preparation

General Requirements

  • Manuscripts must be original, unpublished work
  • Write in clear, concise English
  • Use standard academic formatting
  • Avoid jargon and overly complex language
  • Aim for clarity and precision

Manuscript Structure

  • Title Page
  • Abstract
  • Introduction
  • Methods
  • Results
  • Discussion
  • Conclusion
  • References
  • Acknowledgments (optional)

2. Formatting Specifications

Document Formatting

  • Font: Times New Roman or Arial
  • Font Size: 12 point
  • Line Spacing: Double
  • Margins: 1 inch (2.54 cm) on all sides

Recommended Manuscript Lengths

  • Research Articles: 4000-6000 words
  • Review Papers: 5000-7000 words
  • Case Studies: 3000-4500 words

3. Submission Guidelines

Manuscript Submission

  • Submit through online submission system
  • Provide a cover letter
  • Include declaration of no conflict of interest
  • Ensure all authors approve submission

Ethical Considerations

  • Obtain ethical approvals for research
  • Disclose funding sources
  • Report potential conflicts of interest
  • Adhere to research integrity principles
